# ecommerce-sales-analysis
1. Project Title / Headline
Olist E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product categories, delivery efficiency, and revenue trends of the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset.

2. Short Description / Purpose
This dashboard helps analyze key business metrics such as total sales, products sold, customer ratings, top-selling categories, monthly sales trends, and delivery performance.
The goal of this project is to provide business insights that can help improve sales strategies, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency

3. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
📊 Power BI Desktop – Data visualization and dashboard creation
🔄 Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Measures and calculated columns
🗄 SQL – Data analysis and business queries
📁 Excel/CSV Files – Dataset source
📈 Data Modeling – Relationship building between tables

4. Data Source
The dashboard uses the Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist, which contains real-world e-commerce data from multiple online marketplaces in Brazil. The dataset includes information related to customers, orders, payments, products, sellers, reviews, and delivery performance.

Dataset Tables Used:
olist_orders_dataset
olist_order_items_dataset
olist_products_dataset
product_category_name_translation
olist_customers_dataset
olist_order_payments_dataset
olist_order_reviews_dataset
olist_sellers_dataset

Data Coverage:
100K+ orders
30K+ products
Multiple product categories
Customer reviews and ratings
Delivery and payment information

Source:
Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist (Kaggle)

 * Business Problem
   E-commerce businesses often struggle to track sales performance, product demand, customer satisfaction, and delivery         delays across different product categories and regions.

  The goal of this dashboard is to analyze Olist E-Commerce sales data and provide insights into sales trends, best-selling    product categories, customer ratings, payment methods, and delivery performance to support business decision-making.

  KPI cards showing:
  Total Sales
  Total Products Sold
  Total Products for Sale
  Average Product Rating
  Best Selling Product Categories analysis
  Monthly Sales Volume trend analysis
  Product category revenue comparison
  Delivery performance tracking
  Customer review and rating insights

  Observed monthly sales growth trends and seasonal demand patterns
  Analyzed customer ratings to evaluate product satisfaction
  Detected delayed deliveries impacting customer experience
  Compared sales performance across different payment types and regions
